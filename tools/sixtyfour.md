Sixtyfour is an enrichment platform for people and company intelligence. The current product centers on multi-step enrichment workflows, built-in search spaces, and structured profile outputs that combine contact, social, and proprietary data into decision-ready records.

## Features

- Multi-step enrichment workflows for leads, companies, and research targets
- Search spaces for people discovery, verified emails, phone numbers, and decision makers
- Structured company and lead enrichment outputs with references and confidence scores
- Notebook-style and automation-first workflows for recurring enrichment jobs
- APIs and tutorials for company and lead enrichment inside agent systems

## So agents can...

- Find likely decision makers, verified contact paths, and firmographic details for outreach
- Enrich sparse person or company records before a downstream agent writes or calls
- Build research pipelines that output structured profiles instead of loose web notes
